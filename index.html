Our Story

We Made This Because We Needed It.

It started in a kitchen, not a boardroom. A family member was diabetic. Every "healthy" chikki we found on the shelf was loaded with jaggery, dressed up as wellness. The label said "natural sugar." The blood test said otherwise.

So we did something annoying — we read every label, called every nutritionist, and reformulated peanut chikki from scratch as a clean label snack. Stevia instead of jaggery. Prebiotic plant fibre for bulk. Roasted peanuts for plant protein. Pink salt and cocoa for flavour. Nothing else.

SnackD is India's tastiest peanut chikki, made the healthiest way possible. A sugar free chikki, sweetened with stevia instead of jaggery, high in plant protein and prebiotic fibre. If you've ever put a snack back on the shelf because the label let you down — we made this for you.
